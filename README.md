## 💡 Pro Ordering Tips

1. **Know your size** — Small, Medium, Large (and X-Large on some drinks)
2. **Tell your Brewista your flavor preferences** — they'll suggest the perfect drink
3. **Try the energy drinks** — smoother and more customizable than canned alternatives
4. **Use the loyalty app** — rewards add up fast
5. **Visit off-peak** — early morning or late afternoon for shortest lines
